Gear Selection Tips

Footwear Fundamentals

Choose footwear based on terrain and load. Light trail runners for day hikes, sturdy boots for heavy packs or rough terrain. Always break in new footwear before long hikes.

Backpack Fit

A properly fitted backpack distributes weight to your hips. Look for adjustable torso length, padded hip belts, and load-lifter straps for optimal comfort.

Layering System

Master the three-layer system: moisture-wicking base layer, insulating mid-layer, and weather-protective outer layer. This system adapts to changing conditions.
