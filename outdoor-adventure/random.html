Three Name Bidding System

Enter your name three times for bidding. One will be randomly selected!

1. Submit Bids

How It Works

  1. Enter your name three times (can be the same or different variations)
  2. Submit your bid - it will be saved locally
  3. After all participants have submitted, click "Reveal Winner"
  4. The system will randomly select one name from each participant
  5. The winner is determined by alphabetical order of selected names
You can enter the same name or different variations (e.g., John, John Smith, Johnny)
