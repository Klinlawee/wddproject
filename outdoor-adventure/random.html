How It Works

  1. Enter your full name and three different initials
  2. Submit your bid - it will be saved locally
  3. After all participants have submitted, click "Reveal Winner"
  4. The system will randomly select one initial from each participant
  5. The winner is determined by the highest alphabetical initial
